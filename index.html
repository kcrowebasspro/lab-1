Increasing Monthly Rents after Hurricane Ian

When Hurrican Ian made landfall on September 28, 2022, near the Fort Myers metro area it brough with it catastrophic winds and storm surge. The storm caused extensive flooding in the area, rendering thousands of homes uninhabitable.

Average rents in the remaining housing increased significantly in the months following the storm. Across 17 ZIP codes in the metro area, rents increased by an average of 7% from August 2022 (month 1) to March 2023 (month 8).
